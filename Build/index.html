About Me

I am currently a full time Designer at Made By Cinema - an incubator based in the heart of Soho - where I design for their digital portfolio consisting of Styloko and 23snaps.

During my 3+ years in the industry I have designed for many different platforms including web, mobile, tablets and print. I often joke that I was "raised by Developers" as I was taught to code before becoming a Designer, so I'm also a very capable Front End Developer. As such, my HTML, CSS and JS are pretty sharp, as well as having picked up some Ruby and PHP (among other languages) along the way.

My Work

Mobile

23snaps: onboarding introduction

We wanted to give first time users a little bit of context when they set up a new account. I opted for a popover approach with 3 'slides' to get the job done without getting in the way.

Mobile

Styloko: product pages

These pages needed to contain all of the relevant information about a product, clearly and succinctly. From here, users can add an item to their boutique, or buy it either online or choose from an outlet nearby.

Web

Styloko: trend page

We wanted to create a hybrid shopping/blog experience, so we pulled on our exisiting shopping grid and put it together with editorial pieces and relevant social media to create a home for each trend.
